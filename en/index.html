Nico Litschke

I work at the intersection of systems engineering, safety, risk, and multidisciplinary decision-making in complex, uncertain product development contexts.

Over the past years, I have worked as a safety responsible on software-intensive, safety-critical systems in the automotive domain, including electromobility and AI-based driver assistance systems.

A detailed project history is available upon request. You can find me on LinkedIn or contact me via email at info@nicolitschke.com.

In my texts and on substack, I explore system-theoretical and risk-analytical perspectives on dealing with uncertainty in product development and project work.
